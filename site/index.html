IT capacity is not one budget. It is several different pools with different speed limits.

Business stakeholders often treat IT capacity as a single pool. It is not. If three projects require a security review, and your cybersecurity team can only complete two per quarter, you do not have a prioritization problem. You have a capacity problem.
